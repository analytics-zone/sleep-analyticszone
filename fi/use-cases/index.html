How to Use Health Analytics

Real-world scenarios where Health Analytics helps you make the most of your Apple Health data

📋 Prepare Health Data for Doctor Appointments

Generate professional medical reports that healthcare providers can actually use.

The Problem

You want to share your health data with your doctor, but showing them your phone screen during an appointment isn't practical. Apple's native XML export is too technical for medical professionals.

The Solution

Health Analytics creates clean, professional PDF reports with:

  • Selected metrics relevant to your consultation
  • Trend charts showing changes over time
  • Clinical reference ranges for context
  • Data source information (device provenance)

Common Scenarios

  • Cardiology: Export heart rate, HRV, blood pressure, ECG data for heart health assessment
  • Sleep medicine: Share sleep stages, duration, and breathing metrics
  • Endocrinology: Provide blood glucose, insulin, and nutrition data for diabetes management
  • Sports medicine: Show VO₂ Max, exercise time, and recovery metrics

Quick Steps

  1. Open Health Analytics
  2. Select relevant health metrics
  3. Choose date range (e.g., last 3 months)
  4. Export as PDF
  5. Email to doctor or bring printed copy

Pro tip: Send the report before your appointment so your doctor can review it in advance.

📊 Analyze Apple Health Trends in Spreadsheets

Export your HealthKit data to CSV for custom analysis and visualization.

The Problem

Apple Health shows basic charts, but you want to do deeper analysis: correlations between metrics, custom time periods, or combine with other data sources.

The Solution

Export to CSV and open in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers to:

  • Create custom charts and pivot tables
  • Calculate correlations (e.g., sleep quality vs. HRV)
  • Combine with nutrition or training logs
  • Track progress toward health goals

Popular Analysis Use Cases

  • Weight loss tracking: Correlate weight, activity, and nutrition over time
  • Training optimization: Analyze workout intensity, recovery, and performance trends
  • Sleep improvement: Identify patterns between sleep quality, HRV, and daily habits
  • Chronic condition management: Track symptoms alongside vitals and medications

What You Can Export

  • Steps, distance, flights climbed
  • Heart rate, HRV, blood pressure
  • Sleep stages and duration
  • Weight, BMI, body composition
  • Nutrition and hydration
  • Workouts and active energy
  • VO₂ Max and cardio fitness
  • Blood glucose and insulin

Format: Clean CSV with timestamps, values, and units ready for analysis.

💾 Backup Your Apple Health Data

Create secure, portable backups of your health history in usable formats.

The Problem

Your health data is valuable and irreplaceable. If you lose your iPhone or switch devices, you want to ensure your health history is preserved in a format you can actually access.

The Solution

Regular exports create backups that are:

  • Portable: CSV and JSON work on any platform
  • Future-proof: Not locked to Apple's ecosystem
  • Readable: No special software required
  • Selective: Export only what matters to you

Backup Strategies

  • Monthly full backup: Export all metrics as CSV to cloud storage
  • Quarterly PDF summaries: Create readable reports for personal records
  • Pre-device upgrade: Complete export before switching phones
  • Annual JSON archive: Developer-friendly format for long-term storage

Why Backup Matters

  • Years of health history in one place
  • Protection against device loss or failure
  • Freedom to switch platforms
  • Share with future healthcare providers
  • Personal health research and tracking

Privacy note: All exports are local to your device. You control where backups are stored.

🔬 Health Data for Research and Self-Tracking

Export structured data for personal research, academic studies, or quantified self projects.

The Problem

You're participating in a health study, conducting personal research, or want to analyze your data with specialized tools. You need clean, structured data in a format researchers can use.

The Solution

Health Analytics provides research-grade exports:

  • JSON format: Structured data for Python, R, or statistical software
  • CSV format: Import into SPSS, SAS, or Excel for analysis
  • Data provenance: Know which device recorded each measurement
  • Precise timestamps: Down to the second for time-series analysis

Research Applications

  • Academic studies: Contribute your data to health research projects
  • N-of-1 trials: Personal experiments with interventions and outcomes
  • Quantified self: Deep dives into personal health patterns
  • Machine learning: Train models on your own health data

Data Quality Features

  • Source device identification
  • Measurement accuracy metadata
  • Duplicate detection and handling
  • Custom date range selection
  • Metric-specific filtering
  • Units and conversions included

For developers: JSON exports include full HealthKit metadata for advanced analysis.
