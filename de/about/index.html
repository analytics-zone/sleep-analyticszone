About SleepAnalytics

Part of the AllAnalytics suite. Built by a single developer who wanted the same analytical depth for rest that elite athletes have for training.

Our Story

SleepAnalytics is part of the AllAnalytics suite — a family of apps built by a single developer who wanted the same level of analytical depth for health and wellness that elite athletes have in their sport training apps.

SwimAnalytics, RunAnalytics, BikeAnalytics, WalkAnalytics, HealthAnalytics, and CardioAnalytics came first. SleepAnalytics closes the loop: understanding how you recover and sleep is inseparable from understanding how you train.

We are constantly expanding: new devices, new metrics, new insights. If you have a request, we genuinely read every email and prioritize based on user demand. The Garmin integration, for example, started as a community request.

Our Philosophy

🔬 Transparency over black boxes
You should always be able to see the formula. Every score, every metric — explained.
📚 Science over trends
We cite our sources and use validated clinical standards. No "AI wellness scores" we can't explain.
🔒 Privacy over convenience
We will never make money from your health data. 100% on-device processing, always.
✨ Quality over quantity
One well-built app beats a hundred mediocre ones. We ship when it's ready.
👥 Community-driven growth
Your feedback shapes the roadmap. We read every message. Really.
⌚ Device-agnostic
Apple Watch or Garmin — your data, your device. SleepAnalytics unifies everything through Apple Health.

📬 Suggest a Feature
