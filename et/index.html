Three Reasons SleepAnalytics Is Different

⚡ Same-Day Exercise Analysis — See how today's workout affects tonight's sleep, automatically

No other iOS app does this. SleepAnalytics reads your workouts from Apple Health and correlates them with the sleep session that follows — same day, automatically, for every workout type. After 14 tracked nights, you'll see your personal patterns: which training affects deep sleep, which helps recovery, and when to stop training before bed.

🔬 Clinical Accuracy — Sleep science that cites its sources

Every metric follows the AASM clinical standard. Sleep Efficiency, WASO, SOL, Deep Sleep %, and the Sleep Regularity Index all come from peer-reviewed research. Our sleep score formula is published on this website. No black boxes.

Whether you train with Apple Watch or Garmin (via Apple Health), SleepAnalytics reads your sleep and workout data from a single source. One app, full picture, privacy-first.
